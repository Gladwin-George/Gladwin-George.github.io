SYSTEM AND METHOD FOR AUTOMATICALLY GRADING STORAGE OF ONIONS USING AN ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE ENABLED ELECTRO MECHANICAL PROCESS

Patent No:- 202321032896 A

SYSTEM AND METHOD FOR AUTOMATICALLY GRADING STORAGE OF ONIONS USING AN ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE ENABLED ELECTRO MECHANICAL PROCESS

The invention is a system that automatically grades and stores onions using AI and electromechanical technology. It sorts onions by size (small, medium, and large) and arranges them in separate racks. The sorted onions are then mixed in a rotor motor. A real-time thermal camera and sensors scan the onions to assess their quality, using AI to analyze temperature and grade them. The system also identifies and separates sprouted or defective onions using a mechanical claw.

SYSTEM AND METHOD FOR INTELLIGENTLY ALERTING OVERTAKING MANOEUVRES USING ADVANCED TECHNOLOGY AND ALGORITHMS TO ENHANCE ROAD SAFETY

Patent No:- 202321038171 A

SYSTEM AND METHOD FOR INTELLIGENTLY ALERTING OVERTAKING MANOEUVRES USING ADVANCED TECHNOLOGY AND ALGORITHMS TO ENHANCE ROAD SAFETY

The invention is a system that alerts drivers to perform safe overtaking manoeuvres. It uses sensors and cameras to monitor the position, speed, and trajectory of nearby vehicles. Based on this data, the system provides real-time audio and visual alerts to inform the driver whether it is safe to overtake or not. It also gives recommendations on the required speed and distance for a safe overtaking manoeuvre, helping drivers make informed decisions and reducing the risk of accidents.

SYSTEM AND METHOD FOR AUTOMATICALLY GRADING STORAGE OF ONIONS USING AN ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE ENABLED ELECTRO MECHANICAL PROCESS

The present invention relates to a system (100) for automatically grading storage of onions using an Artificial-Intelligence (AI) enabled electromechanical process. The system and method includes an onion grading module 104 to sort/grade a plurality of onions in different sizes and arranging the plurality of onions in different racks according to the size of onions including small, medium and large. Subsequently, the sorted onions are stored in a rotor motor to mix/combine the onions from bottom. Further, the plurality of onions are scanned using a real-time thermal camera and sensors 108 to determine the quality of onions. The scanning mechanism 108 includes an artificial intelligence algorithm to determine the grade of onions based on temperature and separate sprouted and defective onions from the scanned plurality of onions 102 using a claw mechanism 110.

SYSTEM AND METHOD FOR INTELLIGENTLY ALERTING OVERTAKING MANOEUVRES USING ADVANCED TECHNOLOGY AND ALGORITHMS TO ENHANCE ROAD SAFETY

The present invention relates to a system (100) for intelligently alerting a user overtaking manoeuvres using advanced technology and algorithms to enhance road safety. The system and method utilizes a plurality of sensors and cameras (102) to detect and analyze the position, speed, and trajectory of other vehicles on the road, and provide real-time feedback and alerts to the driver about the safety of an overtaking manoeuvre. Subsequently, the system provides an audio and visual warning to determine the distance and speed of the oncoming vehicle is sufficient or insufficient for a safe overtaking of manoeuvre to reduce the risk of collision. Thus, the system also provides feedback on the recommended speed and distance to perform the overtake safely by enabling the driver to make an informed decision about the feasibility of the manoeuvre.
